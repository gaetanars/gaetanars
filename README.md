### About

Engineering Director at [SFEIR](https://www.sfeir.com), leading platform engineering teams and cloud-native infrastructure across client engagements.

I help engineering organizations ship faster and more reliably by building internal developer platforms — the foundation that turns good teams into high-performing ones.

AI-augmented engineering is at the core of how I work: from intelligent automation to autonomous agent workflows, I apply AI as a force multiplier for platform operations.
